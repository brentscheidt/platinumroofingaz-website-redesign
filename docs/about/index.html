About Platinum Roofing Group.

Arizona roofing since 2010. Residential, commercial, and storm work — all starting with an honest look at the roof.

Foundation

How we got here.

We've been inspecting and repairing roofs across Phoenix since 2010. We started in residential and grew into commercial because clients kept asking us to handle their business properties too.

Approach

Inspection before recommendation.

Every project starts with an inspection. We look at the full system — surface, flashing, drainage, penetrations — before recommending anything. If a repair solves the problem, that's what we recommend.

Standards

The basics, done right.

We're licensed, bonded, and insured. We clean up after ourselves. We show up when we say we will. These sound basic, but in roofing, they're the exception.

How we work

Inspection first We diagnose before we prescribe.

Every project starts with a thorough roof inspection. We look at the whole system before we say anything about scope or cost.

Built for Arizona Heat, UV, monsoons, and hail.

We use materials and methods rated for Arizona's climate. Generic installs don't hold up here — we know what does.

Clean finish Curb appeal matters.

We leave your property looking better than we found it. A clean job site and a quality finish are part of the work, not an afterthought.

Clear communication No surprises.

You'll know the scope, timeline, and cost before we start. If something changes, we tell you immediately — not after the fact.

Who we work with

Homeowners who want honest answers, property managers who need reliability, and businesses that can't afford disruption.

If you own a home and want to know what's actually wrong with your roof before anyone talks you into a full replacement — we're a good fit. If you manage properties and need a contractor who shows up on schedule and documents the work — we're built for that. If you run a business and need roofing done with minimal interruption to your operations — we've handled it before.
