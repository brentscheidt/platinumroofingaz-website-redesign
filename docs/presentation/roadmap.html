Site purpose

This is not a brochure. It's a local-intent conversion system.

The site is built for four specific audiences, each with a different entry point and conversion path.

AudienceEntry pointConversion goal
Homeowners — repair or replacementService pages via organic search ("roof repair Phoenix AZ")Phone call or inspection form
Property managers — commercial roofs/services/commercial-roofing/ or /service-areas/tempe/Phone call + estimate request
Insurance claimants — storm damage/services/storm-damage-insurance/ or blog (/blog/insurance-claim-roof-damage/)Phone call to start claim process
Home buyers — pre-purchase inspection/services/roof-inspection/ or city pagesScheduled inspection booking

A brochure site has one page per service and no path from search to call. This site has 26 pages targeting specific queries — 8 service pages, 8 city pages, 3 blog articles — each with a phone number and form submission above the fold. Every page ends at (480) 974-1595.

On-page SEO — what every page has

  • Unique title tag with keyword + city + "Free Inspection" modifier (e.g., "Roof Repair in Phoenix, AZ | Free Inspection | Platinum Roofing Group")
  • Unique H1 matching the search query in plain language — not a brand tagline
  • Unique meta description with (480) 974-1595 embedded — shows the phone number in SERP snippets before the click
  • Canonical URL pointing to the production domain
  • Internal links to adjacent service pages, city pages, and /contact/ from every page

URL structure — flat and keyword-bearing

Page typeURL patternExample
Service detail/services/[service-slug]//services/roof-repair/
City page/service-areas/[city-slug]//service-areas/phoenix/
Blog article/blog/[article-slug]//blog/insurance-claim-roof-damage/

No parameters, no IDs, no dynamic slugs. Clean, readable, crawlable. This structure will not change at launch.

Performance advantage

Cloudflare edge delivery means pages are served from the node closest to the visitor — not from a shared-hosting origin server in New Jersey. No JavaScript framework, no WordPress plugin stack, no render-blocking scripts. Expected 95+ PageSpeed mobile score versus the 55–75 range for WordPress competitors running Elementor or Squarespace. More than 60% of roofing searches are on mobile. Slow pages lose those clicks.

Content pipeline (AI-assisted)

  • Field capture: Crew uploads job photos to shared folder with one-line note ("Tempe reroof, tile, before/after")
  • AI generates: Caption + 5 local hashtags + CTA + alt text draft in 60 seconds

Review velocity — the biggest trust gap to close

ProWest has 350+ Google reviews. Platinum currently shows 0. This is the most visible trust gap to any visitor doing comparison research. The fix is systematic, not heroic.

  • Post-job SMS sent automatically after job close: direct link to Google review page
  • Target: 10 reviews in month 1, 50 reviews by day 90, 150 reviews by month 6
  • At 50 reviews, Platinum is competitive with Chucktown on Google Maps. At 150, competitive with ProWest.
  • AI monitors new reviews. Response drafted and ready within minutes. Negative reviews escalated immediately.
