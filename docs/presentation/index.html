Why it's built this way

Every design decision serves a purpose.

ElementWhy
8 dedicated service pagesGoogle can't rank one "Services" page for 8 different search intents. Each page targets a specific keyword cluster.
8 unique city pagesWhen someone in Mesa searches "roof repair Mesa AZ," Google looks for pages that specifically mention Mesa. Without one, you lose to competitors who have one.
Cloudflare edge hostingPages load from the server closest to the visitor. Sub-second load times. Expected 95+ PageSpeed vs 55-75 for WordPress competitors.
No WordPress / No frameworkHand-written HTML/CSS/JS. No plugin bloat, no security vulnerabilities, no $200/yr hosting fees. Full control.
Autoplay drone videoReal Platinum footage, not stock photos. Builds trust instantly. Muted autoplay is the standard for hero video.
Single phone number everywhereNAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) is a direct Google ranking factor. The old site had conflicting numbers.
Clean editorial designEvery competitor uses the same WordPress template. A distinct visual identity is itself a trust signal.

Where we win

Structural advantages that competitors can't match quickly.

  • Architecture depth at launch — 26 pages with hub-and-spoke structure. Most competitors built up over years.
  • Performance — Cloudflare edge + no framework. Expected 95+ PageSpeed vs competitor range of 55-75. This is a ranking factor.
  • Page-level SEO — Title tags are keyword-optimized. H1s carry the search query. Content is unique per page.
  • Arizona-specific content — Not nationally-templated copy. Every page references local conditions.
  • City page quality — 8 unique city pages vs Chucktown's 0. Google rewards this for local pack.

Gaps to close

What competitors have that we need.

  • Reviews — ProWest has 350+. We show 0. Fix: post-job SMS flow. 50 reviews in 90 days is achievable.
  • Manufacturer certifications — Chucktown has CertainTeed + IKO. ProWest has Master Elite GAF. These are trust badges.
  • Blog depth — Mission has 14 articles. We have 3. Fix: 2 articles/month puts us above ProWest by Q3.
  • Financing — ProWest offers it. For $8K-$25K replacement jobs, financing removes a common objection.
  • Real project photos — Projects page is built and ready, just needs a photoshoot of 3-5 recent jobs.
