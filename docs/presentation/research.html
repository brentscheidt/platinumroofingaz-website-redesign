The old site

What the research found wrong with platinumroofingaz.com (Squarespace).

Old site — problems identified

  • 5 pages total — Home, About, Services (single page), Blog, Contact
  • Conflicting phone numbers — homepage showed (602) 435-3544, About/Blog showed (480) 974-1595
  • Blog contained placeholder text — "[Your Company Name]" and "H2: 5. Reinforce Roof Edges" as literal text
  • One generic "Services" page — can't rank for roof repair AND metal roofing AND commercial AND inspection from one page
  • Zero city/service-area pages — invisible for "roof repair Mesa AZ" or "roofer Scottsdale"
  • No schema markup — Google couldn't read business type, address, phone, or services programmatically
  • Squarespace hosting — $16-33/mo, slower, template-locked, limited SEO control
  • No conversion path — generic CTAs, no inspection-first framing, no process explanation

New site — how each problem is fixed

  • 26 pages — 8 services + 8 cities + blog + brand pages
  • Single phone: (480) 974-1595 — consistent on every page, verified
  • Zero placeholder text — every word is original, Arizona-specific
  • 8 dedicated service pages — each targets a specific keyword cluster
  • 8 unique city pages — unique content per city, local housing/climate context
  • Schema planned (pre-launch) — Organization + RoofingContractor + BreadcrumbList
  • Cloudflare edge: $0/mo — faster, full control, no template lock
  • Clear conversion path — inspection-first, 4-step process, phone + form on every page
"The current footprint is not structurally built to win local, high-intent search terms because it lacks dedicated service pages, service-area/city landing pages, proof assets, and strong internal linking pathways that funnel visitors to conversion actions." — ChatGPT Deep Research Analysis, Feb 24, 2026

Service page rationale

Why exactly these 8 services. Not 4, not 12.

The research identified 14 high-intent roofing keywords for Phoenix. We grouped them into 8 service clusters based on search intent overlap — each cluster gets its own page because Google needs a dedicated page to rank for each distinct intent.

Service pageKeywords it capturesWhy a dedicated page
Roof Repairroof repair Phoenix AZ, roof leak repair Phoenix, emergency roof repair PhoenixHighest volume keyword cluster (5,000-10,000/mo). The old site's single Services page couldn't compete for this.
Roof Replacementroof replacement Phoenix AZ, roof replacement cost Phoenix, new roof PhoenixDifferent buyer intent than repair — these homeowners know they need a new roof. Different content, different CTA.
Commercial Roofingcommercial roofing Phoenix AZ, commercial roof repair Phoenix, TPO roof PhoenixCompletely different audience (property managers vs homeowners). Different systems (TPO, EPDM vs tile/shingle). Needs its own page.
Metal Roofingmetal roofing Phoenix, standing seam metal roof Phoenix, metal roof cost AZMaterial-specific intent. Research showed competitors like NextGen and BUILT have dedicated metal pages that rank — Platinum didn't.
Tile Roofingtile roof repair Phoenix, tile roof replacement Phoenix, broken tile repair AZArizona's most common roof type. Massive search volume. Research flagged this as a gap — no competitor has strong tile-specific content.
Flat & Foamflat roof repair Phoenix, foam roofing Phoenix, TPO roof repair PhoenixDistinct system type with commercial + residential overlap. Foam recoat queries are unique intent — not captured by "repair" or "replacement" pages.
Storm & Insurancestorm damage roof repair Phoenix, hail damage roof inspection, monsoon roof leak repairTime-sensitive, different buyer psychology ("my roof is damaged NOW"). Research identified this as a distinct conversion lane — all top competitors have it.
Roof Inspectionroof inspection Phoenix, free roof inspection Phoenix AZHighest-converting entry point. Research: "roof inspection" is a real high-intent query and a strong first conversion path. Every competitor with an inspection page outperforms those without.
"The most expensive and valuable roofing keywords are local, transactional, and service-specific. The current architecture makes it hard to rank a single 'Services' page for multiple distinct intents." — SEO Competitive Analysis, keyword gap section

Competitors with location pages (ProWest: 10 cities, Mission: 4 AZ cities) rank consistently higher for "[city] roofing" queries than competitors without them (Chucktown: 0 city pages). This is the single most effective local SEO tactic for service businesses.
