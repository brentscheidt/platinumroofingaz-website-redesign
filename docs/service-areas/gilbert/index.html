Gilbert, AZ

Gilbert roofing — inspections and full-system replacements.

Gilbert has grown faster than almost any city in the valley. That means two very different calls: owners of new builds investigating unfamiliar warranty terms, and owners of early-2000s homes now hitting the 20-year mark on their first roof.

Two generations of roofs

New construction questions and aging roofs that need honest assessments

Early-2000s Gilbert homes used a lot of 3-tab shingle and builder-grade tile that is now at or past its rated service life. Inspections here tend to be decisive — the answer is usually repair or plan for replacement within a few years.

What we look for

Granule loss, deck condition, and whether flashing was installed correctly the first time

Many early Gilbert builds had rushed flashing at penetrations and valleys. An inspection surfaces those details before they become the source of a leak that damages ceilings or insulation, giving you time to plan the scope before it becomes urgent.

How we work here

Inspection findings go into writing, not just a verbal summary

We document what we find, photograph high-risk areas, and give you a written scope so the decision is yours to make with full information. If replacement is the right move, we help you time it, select materials, and manage the permit process.
