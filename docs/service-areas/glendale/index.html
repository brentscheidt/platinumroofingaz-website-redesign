Glendale, AZ

Glendale roofing — residential repairs and commercial projects.

Glendale has one of the most varied property mixes in the valley — older single-family homes that have seen multiple storm seasons, commercial and retail buildings near the sports and entertainment corridor, and multifamily properties that need coordinated access and scheduling.

Older housing stock

Homes built from the 1960s through the 1980s that carry decades of patched repairs

Glendale residential calls often involve roofs that have had multiple contractors touch them over the years. We inspect the full system, not just the surface, and give a clear picture of what is holding versus what is masking a bigger problem underneath.

Commercial and mixed-use

Flat roofs, TPO, modified bitumen, and building access coordination

Commercial properties near State Farm Stadium and the Glendale entertainment district require scheduling that fits around business operations. We plan commercial scopes with access windows, crew staging, and phased work that keeps the property functional while the roof gets done right.

How we work here

Separate lanes for residential repair and commercial scopes

Residential calls route into inspection and repair with same-week availability. Commercial calls get a dedicated walkthrough, a written scope, and a project plan before any work begins. The two tracks stay separate so neither gets treated like the other.
